MiniMechanic
Car Maintenance Mobile App

Helping people easily and effectively manage and plan their car repairs.

What It’s About

This is a mobile app that I worked on as part of my CareerFoundry UI design course. I wanted to design an application for tracking and planning maintenance for vehicles, since it can be a difficult and time-consuming task without the proper knowledge.

Designing the App

Initial Wireframes

Here’s a sampling of screens from the dashboard and vehicle information screens. All the data around a vehicle is separated into three tabs and these would be the screens most commonly seen by the user.

User Testing

After finishing my initial low-fidelity wireframes, I put together a mid-fidelity prototype to use for some informal user testing. I performed the tests with five users. After getting feedback, I made some changes and finished my high-fidelity screens.
