Cash Stash
Mobile and Desktop App

An accessible way for everyone to help save money and manage their finances.

Designed as part of the CareerFoundry UI Design course.

Why Use Cash Stash?

  • Set financial goals and track your progress using easy to read metrics and charts.
  • Modify and track your income and expenses to understand your spending and saving habits.
  • Get advice and tips to make the best of your money and stay on track.

Even Better On Desktop!

The robust and easy-to-read dashboard helps you quickly understand how well you are meeting your goals.
